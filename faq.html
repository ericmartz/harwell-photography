HARWELL PHOTOGRAPHY

What is your style?

My passion is capturing the essence of a wedding: all the emotion, beauty and excitement in a Fine art, fashion and photojournalistic style. I strive to deliver beautiful real moments, editorial style details, and fine art, fashion portraits. I am passionate about using natural light and letting the subject reveal something of themselves to me. I believe this is the essence of great photography, capturing the true personality and spirit of the subject.

Do you shoot film or digital?

My main body of work is digital, but I also love shooting film. I love my Holga, my Polaroid SX-70 and my other film bodies.

What is your processing philosophy?

I like pictures to be as natural as possible, from the capture to the processing and the final printing. Photoshop actions and processing are definitely not my style. I am a purist. Great photography is timeless. I believe your images should not look dated with the latest Photoshop technique.

My prints are archival prints that last 100 years, not machine prints that quickly fade. The are printed on archival paper with archival ink.

My passion is to deliver a beautiful, timeless work of art that you can look at 50 years from now and be moved with the beauty and timelessness of the image.

I would love the opportunity to hear from you.
